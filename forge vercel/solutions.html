Questions

Things customers ask us.

Where does the solution run? +

Our solutions are built natively on Microsoft Azure and the Power Platform, and deploy into your own Microsoft tenant. Your data stays within your tenant boundary and security model.

Do we need an existing Microsoft 365 or Dynamics 365 tenant? +

Yes — the products extend the Microsoft Cloud, so a tenant is required. If you don't have one yet, our Modern Work and Dynamics 365 services can set that up first.

How is the AI in the product handled? +

AI features are built on Azure OpenAI and Microsoft Copilot technologies, governed by your tenant's data and compliance settings. AI is part of the product design, not an add-on.

Can you customise the solution for us? +

Yes. The packaged product is the baseline; our engineering team can extend and integrate it for your environment through an implementation or consulting engagement.

How is it licensed and billed? +

Per user, on a monthly or annual subscription. Pricing aligns to the SOL-series items in our Dynamics 365 price list — talk to us for a quote tailored to your size.
