An engineering partner for the Microsoft Cloud.

Forge T Labs is an AI Cloud Solutions company and a Microsoft ISV partner. We deliver Microsoft platform services — and we build our own software on the same stack. That combination is who we are.

Who we are

Two things at once — by design.

Most Microsoft partners do one of two things. They deliver services — implementing Dynamics 365, Microsoft 365 and Azure for their clients. Or they are software vendors, shipping a product.

Forge T Labs does both, deliberately. Our services practice gives us deep, certified platform expertise across the Microsoft Cloud. Our ISV practice puts that expertise to work building our own packaged software. Each side makes the other stronger — the services prove we can build, and the products prove the services are real.

What we believe

Why we build on Microsoft.

Committing to one platform is a choice. We made it on purpose, and it shapes everything we deliver.

/ 01

Trusted foundation

Everything we deliver inherits the security, identity and compliance of the customer's own Microsoft tenant. We are not asking anyone to trust a new platform.

/ 02

Lower friction

Customers already on Microsoft 365 or Dynamics 365 adopt what we build without introducing a foreign system into their estate.

/ 03

AI as a native capability

Azure AI and Microsoft Copilot are building blocks we design with from the start — so AI is part of what we deliver, not an integration project.
